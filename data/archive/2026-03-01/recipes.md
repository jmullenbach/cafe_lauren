## Pesto Cavatappi with Roma Tomatoes
*Fresh homemade basil pesto tossed with cavatappi pasta, halved Roma tomatoes, and Parmesan*
**Prep time:** 10 min | **Cook time:** 10 min | **Total:** 20 min
**Serves:** 5 | **Healthiness:** 7/10 | **Deliciousness:** 8/10 | **Cost:** ~$8

### Ingredients
- [ ] 1 lb cavatappi pasta (pantry)
- [ ] 2 cups fresh basil leaves, packed
- [ ] 1/3 cup pine nuts (or walnuts)
- [ ] 3 cloves garlic
- [ ] 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, plus more for serving
- [ ] 1/2 cup olive oil
- [ ] 1 pint Roma tomatoes, halved (about 4-5 tomatoes)
- [ ] Salt and pepper
- [ ] Juice of 1 lemon (counter)
- [ ] Lettuce for side salad (fridge)
- [ ] Salad dressing of choice

### Instructions

Make the Pesto:
- [ ] In a food processor, combine **2 cups fresh basil**, **1/3 cup pine nuts**, and **3 cloves garlic**. Pulse until coarsely chopped.
- [ ] Add **1/2 cup grated Parmesan** and **juice of 1 lemon**. Pulse to combine.
- [ ] With the processor running, drizzle in **1/2 cup olive oil** until smooth. Season with **salt and pepper**.

Cook the Pasta:
- [ ] Boil **1 lb cavatappi pasta** in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water before draining.

Toss & Serve:
- [ ] Halve **4-5 Roma tomatoes** lengthwise.
- [ ] Toss drained pasta with the pesto, adding reserved pasta water a splash at a time to loosen.
- [ ] Fold in the halved tomatoes gently.
- [ ] Serve topped with extra Parmesan. Pair with a **simple lettuce side salad**.

## Taco Tuesday
*Classic beef tacos with all the fixings — quick, crowd-pleasing, and customizable*
**Prep time:** 5 min | **Cook time:** 15 min | **Total:** 20 min
**Serves:** 5 | **Healthiness:** 7/10 | **Deliciousness:** 9/10 | **Cost:** ~$16

### Ingredients
- [ ] 2.5 lbs Star Ranch Angus beef taco meat
- [ ] 1 packet taco seasoning
- [ ] 12 taco shells (hard or soft tortillas)
- [ ] 1 cup shredded lettuce (fridge)
- [ ] 3 Roma tomatoes, diced
- [ ] 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
- [ ] 1/2 cup sour cream
- [ ] 1 jar salsa
- [ ] 1 lime, cut into wedges (counter)
- [ ] Hot sauce (optional)
- [ ] 1 avocado, sliced (optional)

### Instructions

Cook the Meat:
- [ ] In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook **2.5 lbs beef taco meat** until browned, breaking it up as it cooks, about 8 minutes.
- [ ] Drain excess fat. Add **1 packet taco seasoning** and **1/2 cup water**. Simmer 5 minutes until thickened.

Prep the Toppings:
- [ ] Shred **1 cup lettuce** and dice **3 Roma tomatoes**.
- [ ] Set out **shredded cheese**, **sour cream**, **salsa**, **lime wedges**, and **hot sauce**.

Serve:
- [ ] Warm **12 taco shells** per package directions.
- [ ] Let everyone build their own tacos.

## Sheet Pan Chicken Thighs & Green Beans
*Crispy-skinned chicken thighs roasted with green beans and golden potatoes — one pan, no fuss*
**Prep time:** 5 min | **Cook time:** 20 min | **Total:** 25 min
**Serves:** 5 | **Healthiness:** 8/10 | **Deliciousness:** 8/10 | **Cost:** ~$6

### Ingredients
- [ ] 2 lbs boneless chicken thighs (freezer — thaw ahead)
- [ ] 1 serving green beans (fridge)
- [ ] 3 lb bag Yukon Gold potatoes, quartered
- [ ] 3 tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 1 tsp garlic powder
- [ ] 1 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- [ ] Salt and pepper

### Instructions

Prep & Season:
- [ ] Preheat oven to 425°F.
- [ ] Quarter **3 lb bag Yukon Gold potatoes** (no need to peel).
- [ ] Toss potatoes and **1 serving green beans** with **2 tbsp olive oil**, **1 tsp garlic powder**, **1 tsp Italian seasoning**, **salt and pepper** on a sheet pan.
- [ ] Season **2 lbs chicken thighs** with **1 tbsp olive oil**, **1 tsp smoked paprika**, **salt and pepper**. Nestle among the vegetables.

Roast:
- [ ] Roast at 425°F for 20 minutes until chicken reaches 165°F and potatoes are golden and fork-tender.

Serve:
- [ ] Serve directly from the sheet pan. Squeeze a lemon wedge over top if desired.

## Quick Skillet Sausages with Peppers & Onions
*Italian sausages seared and simmered with colorful peppers and red onions, served over pasta*
**Prep time:** 5 min | **Cook time:** 15 min | **Total:** 20 min
**Serves:** 5 | **Healthiness:** 7/10 | **Deliciousness:** 8/10 | **Cost:** ~$10

### Ingredients
- [ ] 2 lbs Italian sausages (sweet or hot, or a mix)
- [ ] 2 bell peppers (any colors), sliced
- [ ] 2 red onions, sliced
- [ ] 3 cloves garlic, minced
- [ ] 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
- [ ] 1 tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- [ ] Salt and pepper
- [ ] 1 lb Barilla pasta (penne or spaghetti)
- [ ] Grated Parmesan for serving

### Instructions

Sear the Sausages:
- [ ] Heat **1 tbsp olive oil** in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
- [ ] Brown **2 lbs Italian sausages** on all sides, about 4 minutes. Remove and slice into coins.

Cook the Vegetables:
- [ ] In the same skillet, add **2 sliced bell peppers** and **2 sliced red onions**. Sauté 4-5 minutes until softened.
- [ ] Add **3 cloves minced garlic** and **1 tsp Italian seasoning**, cook 30 seconds.

Simmer:
- [ ] Return sausage coins to the skillet. Pour in **1 can diced tomatoes**.
- [ ] Simmer 5 minutes until sausages are cooked through and sauce thickens. Season with **salt and pepper**.

Pasta:
- [ ] Meanwhile, boil **1 lb Barilla pasta** until al dente. Drain.

Serve:
- [ ] Spoon sausage and pepper mixture over pasta. Top with **grated Parmesan**.

## Instant Pot Chicken Tortilla Soup
*Rich, spiced chicken soup with black beans, corn, and all the Tex-Mex toppings — a family 5-star favorite*
**Prep time:** 10 min | **Cook time:** 25 min | **Total:** 35 min
**Serves:** 5 (+ leftovers for 2 days) | **Healthiness:** 8/10 | **Deliciousness:** 9/10 | **Cost:** ~$14

### Ingredients
- [ ] 2.5 lbs boneless chicken thighs (freezer — thaw ahead)
- [ ] 1 tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 1 large onion, diced
- [ ] 3 cloves garlic, minced
- [ ] 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
- [ ] 1 can (15 oz) fire-roasted diced tomatoes
- [ ] 1 can (15 oz) corn, drained (or frozen corn)
- [ ] 1 can (4 oz) diced green chiles
- [ ] 4 cups chicken broth
- [ ] 1 tbsp chili powder
- [ ] 1 tsp cumin
- [ ] 1 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] 1/2 tsp oregano
- [ ] Salt and pepper to taste
- [ ] Juice of 2 limes

#### Toppings
- [ ] 2 avocados, diced
- [ ] 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
- [ ] Sour cream
- [ ] Tortilla chips, crushed
- [ ] Fresh cilantro, chopped
- [ ] Lime wedges

### Instructions

Sauté Aromatics (Instant Pot — Sauté mode):
- [ ] Set Instant Pot to Sauté. Add **1 tbsp olive oil**.
- [ ] Add **1 diced onion** and cook 3 minutes until softened.
- [ ] Add **3 cloves minced garlic**, stir 30 seconds until fragrant.

Build the Soup:
- [ ] Add **2.5 lbs chicken thighs** (whole, no need to cut).
- [ ] Add **1 can black beans**, **1 can fire-roasted tomatoes**, **1 can corn**, and **1 can green chiles**.
- [ ] Pour in **4 cups chicken broth**.
- [ ] Season with **1 tbsp chili powder**, **1 tsp cumin**, **1 tsp smoked paprika**, **1/2 tsp oregano**, **salt and pepper**.
- [ ] Stir to combine.

Pressure Cook:
- [ ] Lock lid, set valve to Sealing. Cook on High Pressure for 15 minutes.
- [ ] Quick release when done.

Shred & Finish:
- [ ] Remove chicken and shred with two forks.
- [ ] Return shredded chicken to the pot. Stir in **juice of 2 limes**.
- [ ] Taste and adjust seasoning.

Serve:
- [ ] Ladle into bowls. Top with **diced avocado**, **shredded cheese**, **sour cream**, **crushed tortilla chips**, **cilantro**, and **lime wedges**.

### Leftover Ideas
- Day 2: Reheat and serve with fresh tortilla chips and a new avocado — tastes even better next day
- Day 3: Pour over rice for a tortilla soup rice bowl

## Mediterranean Couscous Salad with Grilled Chicken
*Existing family recipe — couscous tossed with grilled chicken, fresh veggies, feta, and a lemon-oregano vinaigrette*
**Prep time:** 10 min | **Cook time:** 15 min | **Total:** 25 min
**Serves:** 5 | **Healthiness:** 9/10 | **Deliciousness:** 8/10 | **Cost:** ~$12

### Ingredients
- [ ] 2 lbs boneless chicken thighs
- [ ] 2 cups couscous
- [ ] 1 cucumber, diced
- [ ] 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- [ ] 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
- [ ] 6 oz crumbled feta cheese
- [ ] 1/4 cup olive oil
- [ ] 2 tbsp lemon juice (counter lemons)
- [ ] 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
- [ ] 1 tsp dried oregano
- [ ] Salt and pepper to taste
- [ ] Fresh parsley for garnish

### Instructions

Prepare the Chicken:
- [ ] Season **2 lbs boneless chicken thighs** with **salt, pepper**, and a bit of **dried oregano**.
- [ ] Grill or pan-sear over medium-high heat for 6-7 minutes per side until cooked through. Remove and let rest.

Cook the Couscous:
- [ ] Prepare **2 cups couscous** according to package instructions.

Assemble the Salad:
- [ ] In a large bowl, combine **1 diced cucumber**, **1 cup halved cherry tomatoes**, **1/2 cup finely chopped red onion**, **1/4 cup olive oil**, **2 tbsp lemon juice**, **1 tbsp red wine vinegar**, and **salt and pepper to taste**.
- [ ] Fluff the cooked couscous with a fork and add it to the bowl. Mix well.
- [ ] Slice the grilled chicken and place on top. Sprinkle with **6 oz crumbled feta** and garnish with **fresh parsley**.

### Leftover Ideas
- Day 2: Serve cold as a lunch salad — the flavors develop overnight
